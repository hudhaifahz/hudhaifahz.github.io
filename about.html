Hello,

Allow me to introduce Vancouver International Trade Institute (VITI). 

Established September 2015, our core focus is academic in nature. We are launching two self-introducing journals this year: the specialized Vancouver International Trade Journal (VITJ) and the more general Vancouver Journal of Law, Business & Economics (VJLBE). Professional services, and soon, courses in arbitration and international dispute resolution will follow.

Below you will find our esteemed advisors, editors and partners who have established themselves in industry and academia around the world. They bring years of accumulated knowledge and wisdom, only in search of more. We appreciate the complexities of an ever more connected world and are here to provide a platform for its recording. Mighty is the pen.

We are positioned to ride out on the Trans Pacific Partnership discussion, and tackle some of the most difficult topics in international trade relations. Vancouver’s diversity and talent base give us a true edge over many other places. Browse our platform and join us for the ride!

Sincerely,

Anowar Zahid
